About the Founder

Arun Mohan K is the founder and developer of KnotSpot, a student-focused social platform built to help college students connect, socialize, and build meaningful campus communities. Originally from Malappuram, Kerala, Arun is a Computer Science and Engineering student at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Ramapuram Campus.

Why KnotSpot Exists

The idea for KnotSpot started from a simple observation: many students enter college without knowing anyone outside their classroom or friend circle. Finding roommates, making new friends, discovering campus events, sharing experiences, and connecting with like-minded students often happens through scattered WhatsApp groups and social media platforms.

Arun envisioned a single platform dedicated to student life, where these interactions could happen in a safer, more organized, and more engaging environment — a true student social platform rather than a patchwork of unrelated group chats.

What KnotSpot Has Become

Built and maintained primarily by a student, KnotSpot has grown into a platform that supports features such as student matching, roommate discovery, anonymous confessions, campus events, voice channels, public drawing walls, student communities, and creator spotlights. The platform aims to bring together students from multiple universities, including SRM, VIT, and Amrita campuses, while preserving a strong sense of campus identity and community.

KnotSpot is not a profitable commercial venture — it is a passion project focused on making student life more connected. The platform is not officially affiliated with any university.

Vision for the Future

Arun continues to actively develop and improve KnotSpot, focusing on creating innovative features that make student life more connected, engaging, and enjoyable. His vision is to build one of India's largest student communities, helping students form friendships, discover opportunities, and create lasting memories throughout their college journey.
